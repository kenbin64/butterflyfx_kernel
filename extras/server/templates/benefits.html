Why ButterflyFX

ButterflyFX treats geometry as first-class data: surfaces and contours carry meaning. Instead of copying and storing every value, you interpret shapes through context lenses and extract what you need on demand.

Key benefits

  • Minimal data movement: data stays where it is until explicitly requested.
  • Geometry-based computing: shapes do the work—points and contours represent structured meaning.
  • Context lenses: different views give different meanings to the same geometry.
  • Universal Connector & SRLs: link resources without copying—apps can operate on remote shapes directly.
  • Universal Hard Drive: a passive, remote resource that behaves like a library card; it doesn't upload until asked.
