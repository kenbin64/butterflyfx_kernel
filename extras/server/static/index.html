What Is a Substrait?

A substrait is a geometric shape whose contours, angles, and density already carry the data— much like a melody carries emotion or a landscape carries depth.

Instead of storing a picture, we keep the shape that can generate the picture. Instead of storing a sound file, we keep the pattern that can produce the sound.

ButterflyFX Core, SRL & Universal Connector

The artifact is the always-open, always-free heart of ButterflyFX. It ships with the universal connector, SRL (Secure Resource Locator), and a dev library that’s available across major ecosystems.

SRL includes a connection table (metadata only) with names and status booleans (connected/disconnected; read/write flags). All PII and credentials are encrypted; the table never exposes secrets—only labels and state.

How We Glean Information

Information is revealed by observing a substrait through a lens. A lens is simply a way of looking:

• A color lens shows height as color
• A sound lens turns position into pitch
• A motion lens turns curves into animation
• A vector lens shows direction and flow

The shape never changes — only how you view it.

✹

What We Can Extract

From a single substrait, we can extract:

• Color — height becomes brightness or hue
• Sound — position becomes pitch
• Motion — slope becomes speed
• Events — inflection points become triggers
• Geometry — curvature becomes structure

All of this comes from one shape. No files. No stored data. Just a substrait and a way of looking at it.

Why This Matters

• No stored data means nothing to corrupt.
• Expressions are tiny and portable.
• Any app can read the same substrait.
• Identity stays in control of everything.
• Everything is reversible and future‑proof.
